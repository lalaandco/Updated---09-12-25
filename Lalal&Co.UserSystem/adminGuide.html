Quick Reference Guide

Print this page and keep near your workstation

📦 Processing Orders

Go to Dashboard → Manage Orders
Click "View" on any order
Review order details
Click "Update" to change status
Add tracking number (if shipped)
Click "Update Order Status"
Status Meaning
Pending New order, needs review
Confirmed Order verified, preparing
Processing Being packed
Shipped Out for delivery
Delivered Customer received

➕ Adding New Products

Go to Dashboard → Add Product
Enter product name
Add description and notes
Select category (For Him/Her/Others)
Set price in pesos (₱)
Enter initial warehouse quantity
Upload product image
Click "Add Product"
💡 Tip: Initial quantity goes to warehouse. Move to display after adding.

📊 Managing Inventory

Two Types of Stock:
  • Warehouse: Stored inventory (not visible to customers)
  • Display: Available for customer purchase
Adding Stock to Warehouse:
Go to Inventory Management
Find product
Click "Add to Warehouse"
Enter quantity
Submit
Moving to Display:
Go to Inventory Management
Find product
Click "Move to Display"
Enter quantity to move
Submit
⚠️ Warning: Can't move more than warehouse has!

🏢 Creating Purchase Orders

Go to Dashboard → Purchase Orders
Click "Create Purchase Order"
Enter supplier name
Set purchase date
Add products one by one
Enter quantity and unit cost for each
Review total cost
Click "Create Purchase Order"
When Stock Arrives:
Go to Purchase Orders
Find your purchase order
Click "Receive"
Confirm receipt
Stock automatically added to warehouse!

🧾 Generating Invoices

Go to Dashboard → Create Invoice
Select order from dropdown
Review invoice details
Click "Print Invoice"
In print dialog: Enable "Background graphics"
Print or save as PDF
💡 For PDF: In print dialog, select "Save as PDF" as destination

📈 Viewing Reports

Sales Report:
Shows revenue and orders
Filter by date range
See top-selling products
Purchase Report:
Shows inventory restocking
Track what you've bought
Stock Report:
Current inventory levels
Low stock alerts
Stock valuation
💡 Tip: Generate reports at end of each week/month

👥 Customer Account Summary

Go to Dashboard → Account Summary
View all customer accounts
See total spent per customer
Track purchase history
Identify VIP customers
Click "View" for detailed info
Click "View Orders" to see order history
Customer Types:
  • VIP: Spent over ₱5,000
  • Regular: Has purchase history
  • New: No purchases yet

✏️ Editing Products

Go to Dashboard → Edit Products
Find product (use search or filters)
Click "Edit Product"
Modify name, description, or price
Click "Update Product Details"
⚠️ Note: To change quantities, use Inventory Management instead!

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid
Don't: Add stock directly to display quantity
Do: Always add to warehouse first, then move to display
Don't: Forget to update order status
Do: Update status at each stage of fulfillment
Don't: Skip adding tracking numbers for shipped orders
Do: Always add tracking info when available
Don't: Receive purchase orders before stock arrives
Do: Only click "Receive" when stock is physically present
